Pizza recipe

Description

Cooking pizza is a labor of love, but the end result is always worth it. Here's a condensed guide to crafting this classic dish:

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (typically mozzarella)
  • Toppings of your choice (such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Olive oil (for brushing the crust, optional)
  • Salt and pepper (for seasoning, optional)

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat oven to 245Â°C
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add desired toppings.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
